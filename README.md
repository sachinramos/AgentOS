## What is AgentOS?

AgentOS is an **AI Fleet Governance Platform** built for teams deploying AI agents at scale. It gives you full visibility and control over every AI agent in your organization — regardless of provider.

Think of it as **HR for your AI workforce**: onboarding, payroll, performance reviews, compliance, and termination — all in one place.

## Features

### Token Payroll
Metered cost tracking per agent. See per-token spend, set budget caps, and get alerts before costs spiral. Compare providers instantly.

### Performance Scorecard
Measure success rates, latency, accuracy, and human ratings. Certify agents that meet your quality bar. Suspend those that don't.

### Governance & Kill Switch
Instant shutdown for misbehaving agents. Blast radius containment, drift detection, and shadow-AI surveillance built in.

### Reasoning Traces
Full transparency into how every agent reached its decision. Audit trails for compliance teams and regulators.

### Drift Alerts
Real-time monitoring for behavioral drift, hallucination spikes, and cost anomalies. Catch problems before users do.

### Compliance Evidence Packs
SOC2-ready audit evidence generated automatically. Export compliance reports for regulators with one click.
